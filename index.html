Never Expire

NeverExpire is an innovative system features a magnet-based latch with four individual clocks, allowing you to track up to four different timed cycles simultaneously. Designed with a bezel mechanism inspired by diver watches, NeverExpire lets you easily align the current day with the 2 week timer, ensuring effortless and accurate tracking. By keeping your food management simple and efficient, NeverExpire helps save money and ensures you make the most of what’s in your fridge.

Problem Space

“I forgot I had this in the fridge! It’s spoiled already!”
Relatable sentiment? Many of us have faced the frustration of discovering forgotten food in the fridge - spoiled, wasted, and headed for the trash. Or worse, forcing ourselves to eat something that’s far from fresh. The challenge comes from juggling multiple expiration dates and losing track in the chaos of daily life. With eating out in Bellevue being pricey, staying on top of fresh ingredients at home should be simple, not stressful!

Gear Train Mechanism

Initially the plan is to create a 1:336 gear reduction train to turn the hour hand of a regular wall clock quartz movement into a 2 week hand. However, we had trouble with the mechanical component and size constraints, so we pivoted to using servos.

Servo Issues

With regards to broken servos, we had to test which servos were functional. Supplying enough current to power 4 servos at once was also a challenge. Finally, with regards to half rotation servos: most servos (including the onboard ones at school) only can rotate 180 degrees. We needed a continuous sweep servo so we had to order that separately.
